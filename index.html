Core Principles of Blackbody Radiation

Wien’s Displacement Law

This law explains the **color shift**. It states that the peak wavelength ($$\lambda_{max}$$) is inversely proportional to temperature (T).

$$\lambda_{max} = \frac{b}{T}$$

Stefan-Boltzmann Law

This law describes the **brightness increase**. The total power (P) radiated per unit area is proportional to the fourth power of temperature (T).

$$P = \sigma A T^4$$
